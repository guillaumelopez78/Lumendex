## 📊 Research Areas

- Invoice payment trends (timing, methods, default rates)
- SMB payment challenges & pain points
- Payment method preferences by country

- Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal)
- Feature comparison
- Market gaps

### Technical Feasibility
- Payment provider API integration
- Invoice generation & templating
- Payment reconciliation
- Multi-currency handling
