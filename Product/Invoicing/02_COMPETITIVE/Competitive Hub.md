# Competitive Hub

Invoicing & payment platform competitive landscape and market positioning.

---

## 🏢 Competitive Landscape

### Invoicing Platforms
- Wave (free, simple, North America focus)
- Zoho Invoice (feature-rich, affordable)
- Square Invoices (integrated with payments)
- FreshBooks (SME-focused, premium)

### Payment Processors
- Stripe (developer-focused, multiple payment methods)
- PayPal (consumer trust, established)
- Wise (multi-currency specialist)
- SumUp (card payments focus)

### Hybrid Solutions
- Xero (accounting + invoicing)
- QuickBooks (accounting + invoicing + payments)

---

## 📊 Analysis Areas

- Invoice creation & customization
- Payment method support (card, bank, BNPL)
- Payment reconciliation
- Reminder automation
- Pricing models
- Regional compliance
