## 🎯 One-Line Strategy

**Invoicing without friction** — Create, send, and get paid in one integrated platform, with automated reminders and multiple payment options reducing payment friction and accelerating cash flow.

---

## 💡 The Problem

**Current State for SMB Owners:**
- Multiple tools: invoicing software + separate payment processor
- Payment delays: 30+ days average payment term
- Manual follow-up on unpaid invoices
- No visibility into payment status
- Integration gaps between invoicing & banking

**Market Opportunity:**
- ~2M SMBs in target markets
- Average invoice paid in 30–45 days
- 15–20% of invoices become overdue
- High willingness to pay for faster payment

## 🚀 Invoicing Solution (Roadmap)

### Phase 1: Smart Invoicing
- Quick invoice creation
- Professional templates
- Recurring invoices
- Custom branding

### Phase 2: Payment Integration
- Multiple payment methods (card, bank transfer, BNPL)
- Payment links in invoices
- Instant notifications
- Payment reconciliation

### Phase 3: Payment Acceleration
- Automated payment reminders
- Smart reminder timing
- Early payment discounts
- Invoice financing integration

### Phase 4: Business Intelligence
- Payment analytics
- Cash flow forecasting
- Customer credit scoring
- Personalized payment offers
