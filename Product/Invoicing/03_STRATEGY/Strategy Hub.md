# Strategy Hub

Strategic planning and go-to-market for **Invoicing** platform.

---

## 🎯 Strategic Vision

**Eliminate payment friction** — Create invoices and get paid faster through integrated payment options and smart reminders.

---

## 🛣️ Roadmap Phases

### Phase 1: Smart Invoicing
- Invoice creation + professional templates
- Recurring invoices
- Custom branding
- Multi-currency support

### Phase 2: Payment Integration
- Card payments (via Stripe/SumUp)
- Bank transfer option
- Open Banking integration
- Payment reconciliation

### Phase 3: Payment Acceleration
- Automated reminders
- Smart reminder timing
- Early payment discounts
- BNPL financing

### Phase 4: Business Intelligence
- Payment analytics
- Cash flow forecasting
- Customer credit insights
- Personalized payment offers
