## 🎯 One-Line Strategy

**Own our banking infrastructure** → Better costs, revenue, speed, and customer experience

---

## 💡 The Problem

**Current State:**
- Dependent on BAAS providers (SWAN/Solaris)
- Limited features & roadmap control
- High operational costs
- Revenue shared with BAAS partners
- Slow to innovate

**Constraints:**
- Multiple platforms across markets
- Complex operations
- KYC/AML/Compliance overhead
- Payment operation costs

---

## 🚀 The 3-Axis Strategy

### Axis 1: Common Product Across 4 Markets
- Single, localized banking product
- France, Germany, Denmark, Netherlands
- Unified customer experience

### Axis 2: Single Efficient Core Banking System
- **Owned banking tech** (no BAAS)
- Single platform vs. multiple
- Optimized operations
- Improved revenue & cost structure

### Axis 3: Deep Integration
- **Banking ↔ Invoicing** integration
- **Banking ↔ Accounting** integration
- **Banking ↔ Payment methods** integration
- Best accounting-banking integration on market

---

## 💰 5 Key Benefits

### 1. Ops Cost Optimization
- Support 4 markets without linear scaling
- Eliminate BAAS premium (expensive)
- Optimize KYC/AML costs
- **Savings:** ~X% of current costs

### 2. Banking Cost Optimization
**Major costs:**
- Card creation & logistics
- Card payment fees
- KYC fees (NL/DE)
- Account fees
- SEPA fees

**Owned platform reduces** all these margins

### 3. Revenue Optimization
**Current:** Share revenue with BAAS
- Interchange: 90% in FR, less elsewhere
- Deposit interests: STR + 9bps in FR

**Owned platform unlocks:**
- Missing 10% interchange
- 100% deposit revenue
- Grows with volume

### 4. Bandwidth & Speed
- Eliminate complexity of multiple platforms
- Focus teams on product innovation
- Faster feature delivery
- Easier scalability

### 5. Freedom from BAAS
- No BAAS limitations on features
- Control our roadmap
- Own customer relationship
- Full compliance autonomy
