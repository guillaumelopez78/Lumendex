**AI-powered cash flow forecasting for European SMBs, transforming financial uncertainty into actionable insights.**

| Property | Value |

| **Project** | Cash Pilot |
| **Timeline** | 2024–2026 (MVP launched May 2026) |
| **Status** | Shipped & Scaling |

| **Key Markets** | France, Germany, Denmark, Netherlands |

---

## 🎯 One-Line Strategy

**Transform banking data into precise, 30-180 day cash forecasts** with zero manual entry, helping SME owners avoid overdrafts and make better financial decisions.

---

## 💡 The Problem

**Current State for SMB Owners:**
- "Will I run out of cash next month?" → Guesswork
- Reliance on accounting firms for forecasts
- No real-time visibility into upcoming liquidity
- Overdraft surprises & missed growth opportunities
- Manual spreadsheets & fragmented tools

**Market Opportunity:**
- ~2M SMBs in target markets
- 60%+ lack any cash forecasting capability
- Overdraft costs: €500–5,000/year per SMB
- High pain → High willingness to pay

---

## 🚀 Cash Pilot Solution (3 Phases)

### Phase 1: MVP (Shipped May 2026)
- ✅ 30-day cash forecast from Shine banking data
- ✅ Transparent calculations (no black box)
- ✅ KPI dashboard (balance, runway, cash-out days)
- ✅ Multi-country support (FR, DE, DK, NL)
- ✅ Mobile-ready design

**Adoption:** 19,000 users enabled | 15-20% activation expected

### Phase 2: Advanced Scenarios (H2 2026)
- What-If planning (salary changes, new contracts)
- Seasonality patterns
- Invoice reminders & follow-up actions
- 180-day extended forecast

### Phase 3: AI-Powered Insights (2027)
- Automated forecasting recommendations
- Lending integration (auto-approve SMB loans)
- Benchmarking (compare to peer businesses)
- Smart alerts & interventions

---

## 💰 Business Model

- **Distribution:** Shine banking integration (white-label feature)
- **Pricing:** Included in Shine core product (no additional cost)
- **Revenue:** SaaS licensing + future lending partnerships
- **Customer:** SME owners (35-55 years old, €100k-2M annual revenue)

---

## 🏆 Competitive Advantages

**vs Accounting Tools** (Xero, Exact):
- ✅ Pre-populated from Shine (zero setup friction)
- ✅ SME-owner focused (simple UX, not accountant-centric)
- ✅ Integrated banking data
- ❌ Less advanced scenarios (future roadmap)

**vs Forecasting Specialists** (Fathom, Flowpilot):
- ✅ Instant setup (Shine native integration)
- ✅ Affordable (white-label embedded)
- ✅ Built for business owners, not accountants
- ❌ Less advanced AI insights (future)
