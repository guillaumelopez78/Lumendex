# Cash Pilot Widget

## Constraints

- Limited space for information ⇒ only a few information can be given to the user
- No possibility to select a date or a time period
- Many other widgets present on the Overview page

## Opportunities

- User can be encouraged to directly go to the Cash Pilot page
- A CTA can be added into the widget
- Information can be passed through a few KPIs/graph

# Possible solutions

## Static KPIs

**Information that could be displayed**

- ~~Current balance:~~ not relevant outside of the Cash Pilot feature
- Projected balance in XX weeks
- Cash Runway
- Supportive communication

**Pro/cons**

- Non visual
- Rigid
- Easy to read/understand
- Straight to the point with 2 KPIs and 1 encouragement sentence
- Precise information
- Possible CTA: ?

## Cash flow forecast chart

**Information that could be displayed**

- Cash flow chart
- Cash In / Cash Out histogram
- Balance curve
- Tooltip for detailed information on hoover

Centred on current day

**Pro/cons**

- Visual
- Limited number of time period observed
- Scale change? Week or month?
- No precise information
- Possible CTA: ?

## Contextual KPIs

**Information that could be displayed**

About one time period only

- Cash In / Cash out
- Net Result
- Closing balance

**Pro/cons**

- Which period to choose from?
- Concrete information
- Detailed information

# Solutions to be tested in with user test

## Static KPIs widget

**Information to have**

- Display first and foremost the “Projected balance in XX weeks” and its amount
    - in EUR or DKK or USD
    - Without decimal number
- Display the Cash Runway as second information and its amount
    - With the same display rules as in the Cashflow Analytics page
- Display an CTA button “Go to Cashflow Analytics”

## Cashflow forecast chart widget

**Information to have**

- Have the Cashflow forecast with:
    - Cash In / Cash Out histogram
    - Balance curve
    - Tooltip for detailed information on hoover
- “Go to Cashflow Analytics”
