# Competition study assessment

# Invoicing tools with cash flow forecast feature

- Exact Online (Exact)
- Lexware
- Xero
- Pennylane

### What do they provide?

Most treasury tools aim to replace spreadsheets with a central, cloud‑based money hub:

- **Live connectivity with bank and accounting data:** real time balance, invoices and KPIs.
- **Automated reconciliation:** invoices are synchronised with the accounting tool
- **Collaboration with accountants:** specific access for accountants
- **Scenarios:** creation/modification of inputs

### How Forecasts Are Built?

Short term forecasts start from **live bank feeds and accounting data, then add budgets or historical trends to create a baseline**. Tools let you adjust assumptions (timing, payment behavior, sales) and run multiple scenarios. Some use simple rule‑based roll‑ups (Lexware), others provide short‑term projections (Xero) or full data‑warehouse 3‑way planning (Exact). Increasingly, providers add ML/AI to improve categorization and payment timing (Pennylane).

### Who do they target?

- Small and medium businesses,
- Freelancers with volume, and fast‑growing startups needing cash visibility,
- Accountants and advisory firms that manage many clients.

### What is their price?

From 10€ to 20€ per month.

# Cash flow forecast tool

- Monitr
- Flowpilot
- Fathom

### What do they provide?

These tools provide cash flow forecasts thank to bank and accounting connections. They display relevant cash-flow dashboards and KPIs. They give the capacity to create scenarios to their users. Personalisation is key for the forecast adjustment and within the custom reporting available. More complicated to set up.

### How Forecasts Are Built?

These tools create short term and long term forecast based on accounting data and banking data that they analyse and project thanks to basic rule and simple AI engines.

### Who do they target?

- Small and medium-sized enterprises across sectors that need clear, daily cash-flow visibility.
- Startups and fast-growing businesses that must stress-test different funding or sales scenarios.
- Accounting firms and financial advisors (especially with Fathom), who use the tools to build reports for multiple clients.
- Companies looking for industry-specific budgeting (Monitr) or Made-in-Germany data protection (Flowpilot).

### What is their price?

Starting plans begin at 20€ per month up to 50€ per month.

Only Fathom and Flowpilot propose complex plans between 400€ and 500€.

# Liquidity management tool = cash flow forecast for CFO of big company

- Agicap
- Dryrun
- FUTRLI (by Sage)
- Spotlight Reporting

### What do they provide?

These tools provide the following:

- **Real time data:** data come from banks, ERPs and accounting systems.
- **Real‑time cash‑flow dashboard:** gives CFOs and finance teams an instant view of liquidity for the semester/year with details possible by month/week/day.
- **Scenario / “what‑if” modelling:** budget scenario possible in order to test the impact of decisions before acting.
- **Multi‑entity / group consolidation:** cash roll-up between entities possible for Agicap and Dryrun. Futrli’s licence‑based model supports many client accounts, effectively handling multi‑entity work.

### How Forecasts Are Built?

All solution start with **actual financial data**, apply **budget or historical trends (AI and rule-based)**, then let the user **adjust assumptions** (timing, growth rates, financing) and instantly see the impact.
The scale of the forecast is first monthly to match quarterly and annual forecast. Zoom is possible on the short term (daily, weekly).

### Who do they target?

- **SMEs / Mid‑size firms (10‑200 employees):** Need a single view of cash, quick scenario testing, limited finance staff.
- **Fast‑growing startups:** Rapid changes in revenue & expenses, require real‑time visibility.
- **Multi‑entity groups / Holding companies:** Multiple subsidiaries, inter‑company loans, consolidated reporting.
- **Accounting & advisory firms:** Need to manage many client cash‑flow models from one platform.
- **Industries with complex working‑capital cycles:** Require detailed AR/AP modelling.

### What is their price?

From 50€ to 1500€ per month. Work units are:

- Number of bank connections
- Number of users
