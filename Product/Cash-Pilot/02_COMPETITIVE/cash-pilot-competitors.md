# Cash Pilot Competitive Landscape

Analyse exhaustive des 20+ concurrents dans les catégories: invoicing, forecasting, et liquidity management.

## 🔴 DIRECT COMPETITORS (Invoicing + Cash Flow)

Même audience (PME/Comptables) + même feature set de base.

### ⚡ XERO

**Positioning:** Accounting tool avec cash flow feature  
**Website:** https://www.xero.com/dk/  
**Targets:** MEs, SMEs, Startups, Tax advisors

#### 🔑 Core Features
- ✅ **Invoicing** complète avec online payments
- ✅ **Bank reconciliation** automatique (21,000+ institutions)
- ✅ **Cash flow forecast** 30-180 jours
- ✅ **Scenario planning** manuel
- ✅ **Expense management** & claims
- ✅ **Integrations** massives (apps marketplace)

#### 💰 Business Model
| Plan | Prix | Features |
|------|------|----------|
| **Starter** | $29/mois | Invoicing + 30j forecast |
| **Standard** | $50/mois | + Dashboard customization |
| **Premium** | $75/mois | + 180j forecast + Expenses |

#### 📊 Threat Assessment
**MAJOR THREAT**
- ✅ Avantage: Plateforme complète, massive traction
- ❌ Désavantage: Forecasting basique (pas d'AI, scenarios manuels)
- 📈 Trend: À surveiller - roadmap cash flow de plus en plus riche
- 🎯 Audience overlap: 95% avec Cash Pilot

#### 🏃 Action Items
- Benchmark features mensuellement
- Monitor roadmap
- Considérer partnership pour invoicing data

- ✅ **Multi-year planning**
- ✅ **Scenario simulation** avancée
- ✅ **AI planning** features
- ✅ **Automatic target/actual** comparison

- €39-399/mois (tiered)

## 🏆 Cash Pilot Competitive Advantages

Vs **Invoicing tools** (Xero, Exact):
- ✅ **Pre-populated from Shine data** (zero setup) vs manual entry
- ✅ **Transparent calculations** (build trust) vs black-box algorithms
- ✅ **SME-focused simplicity** vs accountant complexity
- ✅ **Integrated banking** (Shine native) vs bolt-on integrations

Vs **Forecasting specialists** (Fathom, Flowpilot):
- ✅ **Instant setup** (Shine integration) vs complex onboarding
- ✅ **For business owners** (intuitive) vs for accountants (technical)
- ✅ **Affordable** (integrated) vs enterprise pricing
- ❌ **Less advanced scenarios** (future roadmap)
- ❌ **Less AI insights** (future roadmap)
