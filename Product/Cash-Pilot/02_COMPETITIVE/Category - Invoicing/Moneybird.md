# Moneybird

Positioning: Online accounting software for firms and businesses
Targets: SMEs, Startups, Tax advisors
USP: Ergonomics for micro-entrepreneurs; simple integrations and exports for forecasting.
Website: https://www.moneybird.nl/

# Features put forward

**Nothing about forecasting**

## **Smart quotes and invoices. With a professional look.**

- Your quotes and invoices always contain the correct information
- Use your own personal style with ease
- Allow your customers to pay and sign online

### **Your own professional style**

Create your quotes and invoices in your own professional style. Use your logo, font, and colours, or upload your own stationery.

### **Error-free invoicing**

No more mistakes on your invoices. Moneybird ensures the correct invoice number, VAT rate, and that all important information is on the invoice.

### **Clear workflow**

For example, have your customers sign quotes online, convert them into invoices with a single click, and let Moneybird automatically send reminders.

## **Do your own bookkeeping**

Are you looking for a solution to keep track of your bookkeeping yourself? Check out the features of Moneybird and try it for free!

- Quotes and invoices in your own style
- Track Hours
- VAT returns
- Reports

### **Categories**

Assign categories (general ledger accounts) to your invoices and purchase invoices so that all the invoice lines automatically end up in the right place in your reports.

### **Clear reports**

Gain insight from your reports. When you’re keeping accounts, you want to have clarity to make decisions for your business. Reports like the income statement and balance sheet help you with this.

### **Automatic VAT returns**

It’s possible to submit your VAT return and ICP return directly from Moneybird. This prevents errors when retyping and saves time.

## **Use online payment methods. Get paid faster.**

- Add online payment methods to your invoices
- Turn your phone into a payment terminal or quickly send a payment request via the Moneybird app
- The entire payment process integrated into your bookkeeping

### **Payment link on invoice**

Make it easy for your customer by allowing them to pay the invoice directly online.

### **Phone as a payment terminal**

Let customers pay directly with their debit card via the Moneybird app. You no longer need a separate card reader.

### **Send payment request**

Instead of sending an invoice, quickly send a payment request via the Moneybird app.

## **Purchase invoices clearly organised in one place**

For a complete set of accounts, your purchase invoices and receipts from suppliers are just as important. These are key documents for your accounting. Store them digitally in your records via the mobile app or send them to your unique email address.

### **Scan receipts via the mobile app**

Use the mobile app to add your receipts and purchase invoices to your accounts.

### **Pay your purchase invoices from Moneybird**

Easily pay your purchase invoices directly from Moneybird.

### **Free Scan and Recognise**

Moneybird uses scan and recognise technology, enabling even faster processing of your purchase invoices and receipts.

## **VAT return. In a few clicks**

- Easily make corrections and adjustments.
- Submit your ICP return directly to the Netherlands Tax Administration

Select the period for which you want to file your VAT return, review the VAT overview that we’ve prepared for you, and submit the return. It’s a breeze!

### **Corrections and adjustments**

Moneybird automatically recognises corrections in your accounts after you’ve filed a VAT return through Moneybird. If necessary, you can easily file a supplementary return in Moneybird.

### **ICP return**

It’s possible to submit your ICP return directly from Moneybird. This prevents errors when retyping and saves time.
