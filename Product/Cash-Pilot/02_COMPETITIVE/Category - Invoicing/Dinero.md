# Dinero

Market leaders: Leader
Positioning: Accounting tool
Targets: SMEs, Startups, Tax advisors
USP: Very simple interface for Danish micro-businesses, free/entry-level offering and good local integration (invoicing, banks).
Website: https://www.dinero.dk/

# Features put forward

**Nothing about forecasting**

## Accouting

- Unlimited postings
- Cash journal
- VAT settlement
- Attachments management
- Invoice tracking
- Reminder module
- CVR integration
- Document scan
- Tax test

## Invoicing

- EAN invoicing = electronic invoicing
- Invoice designer
- Electronic payments slip
- Offer module
- Order confirmation
- Import/export contacts and products
- Debt collection
- Subscription billing
- MobilePay
- Invoice as SMS

## Addons

- CRM module
- Auditor and bookkeeper access
- Dropbox integration
- Multi-user access
- MyID validation

# Business model

Free importation of accounts into Dinero

Business models:

- Starting: 0 DKK / 0 euro a month
- Starter+: 245 DKK / 32,79 euros a month
- Pro: 345 DKK / 46,18 euros a month---
