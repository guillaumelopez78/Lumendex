# Features put forward

**Nothing about forecasting**

## For entrepreneurs

### InStap

With inStap, you can easily create invoices and quotes—in the app or from your computer. Your bookkeeper or accountant handles your accounting and VAT returns. You send receipts digitally. This saves you a lot of time and ensures everything is taken care of.

Creating an invoice is a breeze with SnelStart inStap. This includes invoices with reverse-charged VAT. Your invoice or quote is created and sent in just one minute. And a quicker send usually means you'll receive your payment or win your order sooner!

This morning you received a receipt at the gas station. You'll also receive a purchase invoice in the mail. Take a photo (or scan) and upload it to your system in seconds. This way, your purchase invoices and receipts are automatically entered into your system, so you'll never lose them again. Ideal for both you and your accountant.

Get paid faster. Who wouldn't want that? Make it easy for your customers with a Mollie payment link. Directly on your invoice in SnelStart. Your customers pay you with a single click!

The online dashboard provides an at-a-glance overview of your financial situation. You can see your bank balance and how much VAT you owe or receive. You'll also see, among other things, how much money you have coming in from outstanding invoices. From a single screen, you have a clear overview of your company's financial situation.

### inKaart

SnelStart inKaart gives you the freedom to decide what you can do yourself and what you'd rather leave to your bookkeeper or accountant. This way, you stay on top of your finances and your business.

SnelStart inKaart makes bookkeeping easy. Want to spend more time on your business, invoice quickly, and manage your bookkeeping with peace of mind? SnelStart inKaart is the right choice!

Your inKaart package includes a business account with SnelStart Banking, which combines banking and accounting into a single overview. You receive five checking accounts as standard and can create additional budget accounts for things like VAT or profit. Everything is easily managed through the SnelStart accounting software or the Banking app.

Send as many quotes and invoices as you like. The dashboard shows you which invoices are outstanding, and you can send a simple payment reminder with the click of a button.

Create and send unlimited quotes anytime, anywhere. It almost sounds too good to be true. But it isn't! Easily create a quote on-site in minutes, right from your phone or laptop. Send the quote to the customer and quickly convert it into an invoice.

Is your glove compartment also overflowing with business receipts? Or is there an overflowing shoebox under your desk? SnelStart inKaart solves that for you. Take a clear photo of the entire receipt and save it. Enter it directly into your records or save it for later. Throw the receipt away, and your records are back on track.

Want to file your VAT return yourself? SnelStart lets you do it in just a few clicks. Your dashboard will show you how much you owe the tax authorities even during the VAT period. This way, you'll never encounter any surprises. Prefer to leave the VAT return to your accountant? No problem! Because that's the core of SnelStart inKaart: you handle it together

Other products:

- inBalans for accounts receivable management and subsription invoices
- insight for order management and cost centers
- inControl for control over stocks and orders
- intrade for automating all trading processes

## For accountants

Want to process your clients' paperwork faster and more accurately? Want to actually have more time to advise your clients? And file your tax returns more efficiently than ever before? It's easier than you think with the smart features of SnelStart Accountant.

Collaborating efficiently with your clients saves you countless hours each month. You'll work more relaxed and serve your clients better. Moreover, with the Accountant package, you can file unlimited tax returns without hidden costs and generate high-quality reports incredibly quickly.

Our easy-to-understand, **user-friendly software** —for both you and your clients—takes the work off your hands. It allows you to manage your bookkeeping and filing returns comprehensively.

As a bookkeeper or accountant, SnelStart Accountant lets you get more done with less time and fewer people. It also makes your work easier and more enjoyable. Want to make sure your clients also enjoy their bookkeeping? It's easier than you think.

With SnelStart Accountant, your clients have real-time insight. They also use a handy scan & recognize app and a free mailbox.

Effective digitization can be challenging. Yet, it's important because it reduces manual work, allows you to provide better advice, and ensures your customer data is stored securely. This adds significant value for your customers.

With SnelStart, digitization is easy. You're not alone; we're here to help. You'll receive personalized advice and free training on SnelStart, so you can get started quickly.

SnelStart's tax software is fully integrated into the SnelStart Accountant package. This practical and **user-friendly tax return package** was designed by experienced tax specialists (including Wouter Nijssen, see photo).

The tax software has direct links to the Dutch Tax and Customs Administration, and thanks to the integration with the accounting software, the correct data is automatically generated. You also immediately have a tax file, which consolidates all your activities for each client.
