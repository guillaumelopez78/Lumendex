# Feature put forward

Exact proposes many tools in the Financial, ERP, CRM and HR fields for companies.

For Accountants they propose Financial, Office Management and Collaboration.

## Financial tools

### Bookkeeping

**The most complete accounting program**

Exact's comprehensive accounting software gives entrepreneurs, businesses, and financial professionals complete control and continuous insight into their bookkeeping. Exact's accounting software offers everything you need for optimal financial administration and grows effortlessly with you.

- Including (e-)invoicing and automatic invoice processing
- Pay and get paid faster with automated accounts receivable management and multiple bank connections
- More than 10,000 accountants rely on Exact

### Invoice

Send professional invoices with a payment link in an instant, even from your mobile phone. Less effort, faster payments, more time for business. Get started right away with Exact Online Invoice.

- Easily create invoices in your own style
- Sends in just a few clicks
- Get paid faster with the direct payment link

### Project administration

**The all-in-one software for project administration**

With project administration software from Exact, you stay on top of projects and finances. From budget monitoring to cost accounting, you'll always have insight into costs, revenue, and margins. This way, you can work more efficiently and focus on what matters most: the success of your projects.

- Combine purchase and sales invoices under one project
- Insight into costs, revenues and margins per project
- Reconciliation of balance sheet and profit and loss account with project administration

### Subscriptions

**Predictable revenue and a strong customer relationship with a subscription model**

Are you familiar with the subscription moment? It's the best part of the month, when your income starts rolling in. Completely automatically and without any action, the payments are deposited into your account and processed in your accounting. By offering your product or service as a subscription, you create a predictable income stream and a strong customer relationship. That's why the subscription model is rapidly gaining ground.

### Forecasting

Exact for Budgeting & Forecasting is the solution that helps consolidate crucial data from multiple sources and brings reporting and analysis capabilities to the entire organization. Data is automatically loaded from source systems, collected, and consolidated if necessary. Moreover, it's always clear who made changes.

**One reliable dataset**

Forget complex and inflexible spreadsheets. With Exact, you benefit from a complete financial data warehouse. One central place where you'll find all the answers for your planning, consolidation, and reporting. Whatever your accounting standards (e.g., US-GAAP, IFRS, HBG).

**Insight into figures**

For each figure, the underlying transactions and planning assumptions can be traced with just a few mouse clicks. You have complete transparency by drilling down to the level of individual bookings. This applies to both your actual and budgeted figures.

**A clear audit trail**

Add documents and comments to create a complete audit trail, with supporting evidence for every correction. All changes during your month-end or year-end closing are logged in the system. This elevates the reliability of your processes and the quality of your data.

**Self-service reporting**

Powerful wizards support the automated creation of transaction and consolidation data, as well as intercompany eliminations. This even applies to investments with changing ownership percentages. The solution includes closure and disclosure management, with workflows that track and guide every step of the process. You easily define the rules and KPI reporting yourself, without the help of IT or consultants.

**Fast & efficient**

By automating process steps, you can minimize manual work. This means you can achieve a complete and coherent plan much faster. Exact for Budgeting and Forecasting can be rolled out quickly and easily worldwide and supports all steps of the planning process.

**Integrated financial planning**

With Exact for Budgeting & Forecasting, you lay the foundation for professional financial controlling with transparent budgeting and integrated financial forecasting. This is achieved through dynamic monthly, quarterly, or annual planning. Create sub-plans at the cost center and cost unit level. Investments, loans, and inventory are also included in the budgets and forecasts, providing a direct overview of your cash flow planning.
