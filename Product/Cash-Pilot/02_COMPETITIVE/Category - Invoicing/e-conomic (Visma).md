# Features put forward

**Nothing about forecasting**

e-conomic is an API platform meant to link small businesses with their accountants.

### e-conomic addresses all your bookkeeping needs in one intuitive accounting program

The **simple, yet powerful, features** give you complete overview of your company’s finances and can help make your business more efficient and profitable.

**Accountant access advantage**

With e-conomic you can allow your accountant direct, digital access to your books. This makes your collaboration with them much easier and helps them to give you far better service and counselling.

**The power of integration**

The program offers a variety of integrations that let you synchronise with other systems. Integrate functions like inventory management, webshop or payroll systems and experience the advantages.
