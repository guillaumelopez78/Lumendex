# Features put forward

**Nothing about forecasting**

## **Exact Reeleezee VAT return**

Automatically send your VAT return directly from your records to the Tax Authority. That's another convenience of Exact Reeleezee. All receipts and invoices you enter are automatically processed into your return. At the end of the month (or quarter), your return is ready for submission in your "Taxes" folder. This also applies if you trade with foreign countries. An orange icon automatically appears in the VAT return menu when a return is ready for you! So you no longer need to log in separately to the Tax Authority's website. It couldn't be easier!

## **Exact Reeleezee bank link**

Exact Reeleezee connects with all banks. It also has an automatic integration with ING, Rabobank, ABN Amro, and Knab. To process all bank deposits and withdrawals directly into your accounting system, Exact Reeleezee allows you to import bank transactions from almost any Dutch bank.

This way, you'll never have to manually enter all that important data again, and you'll have all your records in one convenient overview, organized by folder and general ledger account. You can also immediately see which deposits and withdrawals can be linked to outstanding invoices or scanned purchase receipts.

## **Inbox: Scan & recognize**

One place to store all your purchase invoices and receipts: the Exact Reeleezee Inbox. The Scan & Recognize software reads the invoice and automatically creates a clear booking proposal. Supplier, amounts, and booking categories are all processed automatically. All you have to do is check it. You can even configure invoices that are fully scanned to be booked immediately. So, save time and prevent errors. And you have all your expenses in one digital file.

## **Real easy time registration with Exact Reeleezee**

Want to track your time quickly and easily? Simply enter the hours worked, link them to a project, client, or employee, and convert them into an invoice with a single click.

## Send quotes and invoices quickly, professionally and really easily

With Exact Reeleezee, you can quickly and easily create quotes in your own style. And if your customer accepts the quote, you can convert it into an invoice in a flash.
