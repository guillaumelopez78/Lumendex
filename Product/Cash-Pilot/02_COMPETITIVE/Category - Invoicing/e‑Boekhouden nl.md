# e‑Boekhouden.nl

Positioning: Online accounting software
Targets: SMEs, Tax advisors
USP: Affordable offer for small organisations, exports/reports that can be used to build forecasts
Website: https://www.e-boekhouden.nl/

# Feature put forward

**Nothing about forecasting**

e-Boekhouden.nl offers everything you need: bookkeeping, invoicing, quotes, time tracking, relationship management, inventory management, and much more. As an e-Boekhouden.nl customer, you also benefit from innovative features like automatic bank integrations, scan & recognize, email & recognize, and an app for your smartphone or tablet.

**By entrepreneurs, for entrepreneurs**

e-Boekhouden.nl was created with an entrepreneur's perspective in mind. You'll find the input screens, menu options, and overall user experience intuitive. You can also seamlessly customize input screens and menu options to your specific needs.

- Automatic accounting
- Bookkeeping
- Billing
- Quotes
- Relationship management
- Time registration
- Scan & Mail & Recognize
- Digital archive
- Mobile app

# Business model

**4 different tiers:**

- Starters advantage
    - For starting entrepreneurs.
    - **15 months: FREE**
- Self-employed Package
    - Bookkeeping and invoicing with a maximum number of bookings.
    - ~~9.95~~ **4.98/month** Save €4.98
- Standard
    - Unlimited accounting.
    - ~~14.50~~ **7.25/month** Save €7.25
- Standard + Invoicing
    - Unlimited accounting and invoicing.
    - ~~24.00~~ **12.00/month** Save €12.00---
