# Pleo

Positioning: Card and expense management tool
Targets: MEs, SMEs, Startups
USP: Real-time visibility of business expenses and automatic reconciliation; improves visibility of operational cash flow.
Website: https://www.pleo.io/

# 19/02/2026 Feedback from Laurits Langberg (ex-Pleo)

### USP of the product

- Simple as possible for the employee to do their job
- Cross market
- Accounting tool connections
- Easy to use for Expenses team

### Main features

- Pleo is not a full fledge bank: only possesses an e-money licence
- Provide business physical and virtual cards
- Allow expense management:
    - Take a picture of the expense
    - Capture all relevant information
    - Register all information for finance team to validate
- Business card backed to the company account: KYC only done at company level
- Expenses dashboard, comparison with the past
- Roadmap: create an expense forecast, purchase invoice with scheduling

### Main feedbacks

- Finance teams love it
- CFO can get a live view of all expenses
- You can obtain a card at the same time as you spend

### Targets

- Small businesses then getting to bigger customers
- Pan-European solution with main countries (US possible if company has EU headquarter):
    - UK
    - Germany
    - Sweden
    - Denmark
    - Spain

### Price

- Competitive compare to regular banks: limitation on extra fees, intermediary fees, etc.
- Tool can replace up to 3 FTE

# Features put forward

## Payment

Empower employees to get what they need to excel at their jobs with next-generation corporate cards. Meanwhile, the Finance team handles supplier invoices, reimbursements, and more.

### Next generation business cards

Pleo's physical, temporary, virtual and vendor cards enable safer spending for all teams.

### Invoices

Capture, process, approve, pay and account for your invoices with our automated accounts payable solution.

### Refunds

Easily track, verify and manage reimbursements between employees and the company.

## Management

Gain complete visibility into your company's expenses. Benefit from tracking of every entity, office, recurring payment, and integration, all within a convenient platform.

### Multiple entities

A single solution and a single account for all expenses and all entities. Nothing gets lost in the shuffle.

### Integrations

Connect Pleo to the tools your team uses daily, including NetSuite, Xero and Sage.

### Expenditure control

Take control of company spending with custom spending limits, multi-step approval, budgets and more.

## Optimization

Make more informed purchasing decisions and spot opportunities to save money with accurate, automated reports and detailed insights.

### Automation

Eliminate the stress of expense management by automating tedious expense reports and the hunt for receipts.

### Analytical monitoring

Stay on top of your company's finances with detailed spending insights in a single dashboard.

### Subscriptions

Track all your recurring payments in one place. We make it easy to spot duplicates so you can save money.

# Business model

# Starter

- €4 per month
- Includes 1 user (up to 3 users), €5 per additional user each month
- The advantages of Starter:
    - Physical and virtual maps
    - Accounts payable
    - Real-time expense tracking
    - Automated expense reports
    - Integrations with accounting systems
    - Live chat support
    - 1 supplier card

# Essential

- €18 per month
- 1 user included, €11 per additional user each month
- All the benefits of Starter, plus:
    - Refunds
    - Mileage allowance
    - Flexible card limits
    - Up to 25 supplier cards
    - Supplier management
    - Automated verification processes
    - Credit limit up to €20,000 for eligible customers

# Advanced

- €99 per month
- 3 users included, €15 per additional user each month
- All the benefits of Essential, plus:
    - **0.5% Cashback***
    - Multi-entity management
    - Advanced controls for maps
    - Up to 100 supplier cards
    - Budgets
    - HRIS Integrations
    - Telephone support
    - Credit limit up to €250,000 for eligible customers

# Beyond

- €199 per month
- 3 users included, €18 per additional user each month
- All the advantages of Advanced, plus:
    - **0.75% Cashback***
    - AI-powered expense verification
    - Analysis of spending
    - Up to 10 sub-accounts
    - Purchase orders
    - Guided implementation
    - Personalized assistance
    - Dedicated Customer Success Manager.
    - Credit limit up to €500,000*---
