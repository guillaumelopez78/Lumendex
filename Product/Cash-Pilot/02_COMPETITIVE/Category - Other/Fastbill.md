# Fastbill

Positioning: Invoicing and accounting tool
Targets: SMEs, Startups, Tax advisors
USP: Your life is too short for paperwork and accounting
Website: https://www.fastbill.com/

# Feature put forward

**Nothing about forecasting**

### **Write and send invoices**

Quickly create professional invoices or [e-invoices](https://www.fastbill.com/funktionen/e-rechnung) in your own design and send them to your customers with one click via email or post.

### **Capture and categorize receipts**

FastBill recognizes text and metadata from your receipts and learns as you categorize them. Or you can let FastBill process your receipts completely.

### **Manage customers**

You receive helpful data such as payment history and can quickly find all important information about your customers.

### **Collect evidence**

Capture receipts however you like: automatically from Amazon, DHL and 10,000 other portals, via scan app, [email inbox](https://www.fastbill.com/inbox) or file upload.

### **Banking with payment reconciliation**

All your accounts are clearly displayed in a timeline. Our intelligent payment matching also suggests matching receipts and invoices. Or you can let FastBill handle the entire matching process.

### **VAT return and financial analysis**

Submit your preliminary VAT return (UStVA) directly to the tax office via FastBill, without using ELSTER. Based on your invoices and receipts, FastBill automatically completes your preliminary VAT return for accrual-based accounting.
