# SumUp (Debitoor)

Positioning: Collection and Invoicing tool
Targets: MEs, SMEs
USP: Invoicing should not be time-consuming or complicated. Stop wasting time! Create invoices and quotes in the blink of an eye, get paid quickly, and manage your sales with a simple yet powerful tool.
Website: https://www.sumup.com/fr-fr/factures/

# Feature put forward

**Nothing about forecasting**

## Intuitive invoicing

No more need for Excel spreadsheets or calculators. The system automatically calculates totals and detects errors or missing information.

## Get paid your way

Simplify the payment process for your customers and get paid faster. Accept online card payments, bank transfers, cash payments, and payments via Google Pay or Apple Pay.

## Track your sales

Tired of spreadsheets? Do your accounts and view documents related to each customer, including their outstanding balance, with ease.

## Real-time invoice status

You will receive an instant mobile push notification once your invoice has been viewed, paid, or in the event of late payment. Send payment reminders for overdue invoices automatically.

## All your documents in one place

Keep track of all your sales documents with easy-to-use software. Quotes, credit notes, delivery notes, recurring invoices: anything is possible with SumUp!

## Invoice wherever you are

Are you travelling far from your office? No worries: create compliant invoices and quotes in the blink of an eye on the mobile app.
