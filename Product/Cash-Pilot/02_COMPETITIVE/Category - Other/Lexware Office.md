# Lexware Office

Positioning: Invoicing, accounting and payroll with cash management feature
Targets: MEs, SMEs, Startups
USP: With Lexware, you have accounting, invoices and finances under control, so you can concentrate on your passion.
Website: https://www.lexware.de/

# Feature put forward

## **Writing invoices is that easy**

With Lexware Office's invoicing software, you can create quotes, e-invoices, and payment reminders directly online . Thanks to automated processes , you save time, reduce errors, and always work in compliance with legal requirements . Whether in the office, on the go, or working from home – with this software, you always have your quotes and invoices at your fingertips and can track payments instantly.

- Create (e-)invoices and quotes digitally
- Automatically reconcile payments
- Work remotely anytime

## **Dashboard: Everything at a glance**

The dashboard allows you to keep track of your finances, accounts and tasks at all times, making it easy to manage your planning.

- Keep track of your financial situation, accounts and tasks on the dashboard
- Use income-expense comparison and financial planning
- Securely connect to the bank and assign sales.

## **Capture receipts in no time**

Document capture in three steps – thanks to Lexware Office, it's no problem at all. See for yourself!

- Digitize and securely store receipts
- Automatic text recognition (OCR) for correct bookings
- Order and overview through central filing in Lexware Office

### **Lexware Office Banking**

With Lexware Office Multibanking, you fulfill your recording obligations effortlessly and maintain a complete overview. 

- Multibanking for all account types in one interface
- Automatic allocation and posting of all sales
- Standing orders directly integrated

### **Liquidity planning**

With Lexware Office you get more planning security for your company: forecasts and analyses show you at any time how your finances are developing and where action is needed. This allows me to easily plan sales and costs and compare the actual development with my forecast.

- Income and expenses always in view
- Forecasts for reliable liquidity planning
- Make sound financial decisions

# Business model

4 prices models from 7,9 euros a month to 32,9 euros a month. The two most expensive models includes the forecasting feature.---
