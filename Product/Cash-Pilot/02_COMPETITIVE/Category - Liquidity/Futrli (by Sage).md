# Futrli (by Sage)

Positioning: Cash flow management tool
Targets: MEs, SMEs, Tax advisors
USP: Automated reports, financial forecasts, and insights (machine learning) to aid decision-making.
Website: https://www.futrli.com/

# Features put forward

## **Performance insights**

**Get instant insights in the performance area**

Maximize the value of your time. Uncover crucial insights tailored to your accounting needs, shedding light historical, present, and future performance for your practice and your clients.

## **Reporting and dashboards**

**Fast and professional reports for every stakeholder**

Elevate your strategic discussions with your clients, team, board, or investors using reports crafted for accountants – easy to compile, a breeze to share, and fully customizable to suit your financial expertise.

## **Short term cash flow**

**Get a grip on today’s bank balance**

Master short-term cash dynamics. Understand how payment dates impact cash flow for sharper financial management for you and your clients.

## **Instant smart budgets**

**Next-level variance analysis: Futrli Budgets and Scenarios**

Elevate your variance analysis game. Explore integrated graphs and charts that effortlessly track Budgets and Scenarios, all within a single, user-friendly platform designed for accountants.

## **3-way forecasting**

**Understand how your profit affects cash flow and future business value**

Amplify your decision-making progress and contribute to business growth with targeted future insights. Bid farewell to tedious spreadsheets and embrace our accountant-friendly real-time 3-way forecasting and prediction platform.

## **Customisable chart of accounts**

**Accurately represent your business position within your chart of accounts**

Structure your financial data in a way that makes the most sense for your practice and your clients.
