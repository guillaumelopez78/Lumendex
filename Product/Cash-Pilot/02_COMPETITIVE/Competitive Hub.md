## 🏢 Competitive Landscape

Cash Pilot operates in **3 competitive categories:**

### 1. Liquidity Management Tools
**Profile:** Enterprise/CFO-focused, real-time data, at-scale KPIs  
**Competitors:** [[Category - Liquidity/Agicap.md|Agicap]], [[Category - Liquidity/Dryrun.md|Dryrun]], [[Category - Liquidity/Futrli (by Sage).md|Futrli (by Sage)]]  
**Our Position:** NOT a direct competitor (different audience)

### 2. Cash Flow Forecasting Tools
**Profile:** Accountant/SME-focused, scenario creation, forecast personalization  
**Competitors:** [[Category - Forecasting/Monitr.md|Monitr]], [[Category - Forecasting/Flowpilot.md|Flowpilot]], [[Category - Forecasting/Fathom.md|Fathom]]  
**Our Position:** INDIRECT competitor (overlapping audience, different depth)

### 3. Invoicing Tools with Cash Flow Feature
**Profile:** SME/Accountant-focused, live data, simplified setup  
**Competitors:** [[Category - Invoicing/Exact Online (Exact).md|Exact Online]], [[Category - Invoicing/Xero.md|Xero]], [[Category - Invoicing/Moneybird.md|Moneybird]], [[Category - Invoicing/Pleo.md|Pleo]]  
**Our Position:** DIRECT competitor (same audience, integrated approach)

## 🎯 Key Insights

**Our Differentiation:**
- Pre-populated from existing Shine data (no setup)
- Transparent, clear calculations (trust)
- SME-focused simplicity
- Integrated invoicing + banking approach

**Main Threats:**
- Accounting-focused tools (deeper features)
- Enterprise tools (richer visualization)
- Pure forecasting tools (specialized expertise)
