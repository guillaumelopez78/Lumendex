# Flowpilot

Positioning: Independant cash flow planning tool
Targets: SMEs, Startups, Tax advisors
USP: With just a few clicks, you can connect your bank and accounting system and plan your cash flow for the future
Website: https://flowpilots.de/

# Features put forward

## Connections

Flowpilot connects your bank accounts and your accounting system. You have 100% transparency from the start and can see your cash flow on a daily basis.

## Cash flow

Liquidity plan online, scenarios, target/actual comparison, planning with artificial intelligence.

- Daily cash flow from any number of bank accounts
- Connect your accounting system for detailed analysis of your financial data
- Custom data structure via drag & drop
- Interactive cash flow graph
- Planning and managing cash flow

## **Planning deposits and withdrawals**

- Create cash flow budgets
- Multi-year planning
- Simulating decisions in scenarios
- Data export to Excel
- Automatic target/actual comparison

## **Upload and setup**

- Plug-and-play thanks to the bank interface
- Fast upload of your accounting data
- Start working immediately in familiar structures
- DATEV, bank and CSV interface
- Invite tax advisors and colleagues

## Made in Germany

Flowpilot was developed by German financial experts. The Software as a Service is hosted in Germany.

## Security and data protection

- Enhanced SSL Security
- Data security according to German banking standards
- Software Hosted and Made in Germany
