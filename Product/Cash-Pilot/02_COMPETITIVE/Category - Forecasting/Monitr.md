# Monitr

Positioning: Cash flow management tool
Targets: SMEs, Startups, Tax advisors
USP: Automated, real‑time cash‑flow forecasting that combines invoice data and bank feeds to deliver actionable alerts and prescriptive recommendations in an easy, non‑technical interface.
Website: https://www.monitr.be/

# Features put forward

## **Make a reliable cashflow forecast *in the long and short term***

Monitr employs budget modules to fine-tune the accuracy of your budget, offering you a realistic view of your financial figures. This feature enables you to simulate at any moment the inflow and outflow of your cashflow and project how your cash balance will evolve in the future, in real-time.

## **Calculate every part of your cash flow forecast with budget modules**

Thanks to budget modules, you can create a cashflow forecast in just a few clicks. Furthermore, a budget module adapts specifically to your company's industry. Configure your budget module via a step by step configurator and view the result on your forecast in real-time.

- Make use of **operational data**
- Optimized on **for your sector**
- Best practice **cashflow calculation model**
- Update your numbers and forecast automatically **in real-time**

## Track and manage your figures in real-time

With Monitr, you can easily centralize all your data in one platform. Connect Monitr to your accounting, CRM and operational tools and update your cashflow forecast automatically with the latest data.

- **Top down:** Navigate from a holistic perspective to invoice level
- **Aging analysis:**  Create a report of unpaid invoices, including customer details, amounts due, and outstanding periods.
- **Real-time cash flow forecasting:**  Simulate the evolution of your cashflow and compare performance to your budget in real-time
- **Budget builder:** Compose advanced scenarios in a few clicks and compare your performance against your budget in real-time.

## Import and export with Google Sheets & Microsoft Excel

Monitr is the perfect spreadsheet companion, and it works both ways. Upload your data to Monitr, import your reporting structure, or export your data to spreadsheets.

## **Make use of a *profit & loss sheet*, a *balance sheet* and a *cashflow statement***

Monitr offers a drag and drop system to create a customized reporting structure on the fly. Compare your performance against your proposed budget in real time and automatically update your figures and your reporting structure.

- **Drill-down navigation:** Navigate from a holistic perspective to invoice level
- **Consolidate your data:** Filter by entity or use analytical axes
- **Budget actual insight:** Get a real-time insight into your accounts
- **Forecast insight:** Get real-time insight into your performance your proposed budget
- **Delta insight:** Compare the difference between the performance of your actuals to your proposed budget
- **Mapping:** Choose how you want to include, break down and customize your results in your reporting structure
