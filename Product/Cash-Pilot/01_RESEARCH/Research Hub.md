# Research Hub

Complete user research and market insights for **Cash Pilot** MVP.

---

## 📊 Research Summary

**Sample:** 21 interviews across 4 countries (France 11, Denmark 4, Germany 3, Netherlands 3)  
**Method:** Semi-structured interviews with small business owners (0–9 employees)  
**Timeframe:** Pre-launch validation (completed Q1 2026)  
**Key Finding:** Business owner needs are consistent across all EU geographies

---

## 👥 User Personas (3 Jobs-to-Be-Done)

| Persona | Size | Core Need | Key Decision |
|---------|------|-----------|--------------|
| **Survivalist** | 0 employees | Immediate survival, tax, VAT, bill coverage | "Am I doing good next month? How much VAT next period?" |
| **Tax & Accounting Expert** | 1–9 employees | Advanced info to optimize financials | "Should I stop working this year? How to optimize long-term?" |
| **Builder** | 1–9 employees | Growth & scalability, runway visibility | "Can I hire? Invest? Do I have runway?" |

---

## 🔴 Top 5 Pain Points (Ranked by Severity)

1. **Can't understand cash flow coming weeks** 💹 → Need: 30-60 day visibility
2. **Don't know VAT amount to pay** ➗ → Need: Real-time VAT calculation
3. **Don't know if liquidity allows investing/hiring** ❓ → Need: Cash reserve clarity
4. **Don't know income tax amounts** 🧮 → Need: Tax estimation
5. **Don't know seasonal patterns** 🗓️ → Need: Seasonality insights

---

## 💡 UX Principles Identified

1. **Comprehension** — Clear, jargon-free communication
2. **Trust** — Transparent calculations, no black boxes
3. **No Setup** — Pre-populated from Shine banking data
4. **Transparency** — Show all assumptions & sources
5. **Supportive Communication** — Helpful tone, non-judgmental
