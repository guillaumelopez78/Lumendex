# User Stories

<aside>
👨‍🎤

## 01 : **Survival & Short-Term Clarity**

### User 1 : The 90-Day Horizon

- **As a:** business owner with 2 employees
- **I want:** to see a visual projection of my bank balance for the next 90 days based on my current balance and pending invoices
- **So that:** I don't have to do mental math to know if I can cover payroll next month.
- *Acceptance Criteria:* Must include "Expected Payment Dates" for invoices, not just "Due Dates."

### User 2 : The VAT/Tax "Safety Net”

- **As a:** VAT-registered business
- **I want:** the system to automatically calculate and "reserve" my estimated tax liability from my projected balance
- **So that:** I don't accidentally spend money that actually belongs to the tax authorities.
- *Acceptance Criteria:* User can see a "Spendable Balance" vs. "Total Balance."
</aside>

<aside>
👷‍♀️

## 02 : **Proactive Alerts & Expense Control**

### User 3 : **Expense "Leakage" View**

- **As a** cost-conscious owner
- **I want** a clear view of my recurring business expenses vs. my earnings
- **So that** I can identify "vampire" subscriptions or overheads that are draining my liquidity.
- *Acceptance Criteria:* Clear visual insight into cash inflows and expenses, especially upcoming expenses.

### User 4 : The Early Warning

- **As a** busy founder
- **I want** to receive a notification X days before a predicted cash shortfall
- **So that** I have time to chase late payments or delay a non-essential purchase.
- *Acceptance Criteria:* Alerts must be triggered by *projected* balances, not just current ones.
</aside>

<aside>
👩‍⚖️

## 03 : **Growth & Strategic Planning**

### User 5 : Hiring/Investment Simulator (What-If)

- **As a:** "Builder" looking to grow to 5 employees
- **I want:** to toggle a "New Hire" scenario that adds a recurring salary expense to my 24-month forecast
- **So that:** I can see exactly which month my cash flow would dip into the red if I hire today.
- *Acceptance Criteria:* User can add/remove "Draft Expenses" without affecting actual accounting books.

### User 6 : **Seasonality Insights**

- **As a:** business owner in a seasonal industry (e.g. exports, carpentry etc.)
- **I want:** to see a 2-year forecast that accounts for my historical "slow months"
- **So that:** I can build up a cash reserve during peak season to survive the winter.
- *Acceptance Criteria:* System must identify year-over-year trends from at least 12–24 months of historical bank data.
</aside>

<aside>
🧑‍🌾

## 04 : **Data Logic & Transparency**

### User 7 : **The "Audit Trail" Need**

- **As a** skeptical user
- **I want** to have a clear understanding of where calculations come from
- **So that** I can trust and verify the calculation logic.
- *Acceptance Criteria:* User can click on any point in the future forecast to see a list of the specific transactions (invoices, bills, or estimates) that comprise that total
</aside>
