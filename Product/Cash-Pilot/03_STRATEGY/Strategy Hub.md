# Strategy Hub

Product roadmap, go-to-market, and success metrics for **Cash Pilot** MVP.

---

## 🎯 Strategic Focus (MVP Phase)

**Goal:** Solve top 2 user pain points:
1. **Can't understand cash flow coming weeks** → 30-60 day rolling forecast
2. **Don't know VAT to pay** → Automated VAT reserve calculation

**Scope:** Q2 2026 launch via silent release (May 19, 2026)

**Initial Users:** 19,000 (Shine users with connected bank accounts)

## 💡 Strategic Insights

**Why Now:**
- SMBs lack cash flow visibility
- Growing demand for integrated forecasting
- Shine has user base & data infrastructure

**Competitive Advantage:**
- No setup (pre-populated from Shine)
- Trust through transparency
- Integrated approach (invoicing + banking)
- Europe-wide (4 countries validated)

**Key Success Factors:**
- User adoption (first 30 days critical)
- Forecast accuracy (trust)
- Simplicity (no learning curve)
