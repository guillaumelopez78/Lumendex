# Competitive Hub

Accounting software competitive landscape and market positioning.

---

## 🏢 Competitive Landscape

### Major Accounting Software
- Xero (global, cloud-native, SME-focused)
- QuickBooks (US-dominant, growing internationally)
- FreshBooks (invoicing + accounting)
- Wave (free tier, limited)

### Regional Players
- Sage (Europe, enterprise-focused)
- Exact Online (Netherlands, Belgium)
- Tripleseat (event-focused)

### Adjacent Tools
- Invoice generators (Zoho Invoice, Square Invoices)
- Receipt scanners (Expensify, Shoeboxed)
- Bank connectors (Plaid, Open Banking APIs)

---

## 📊 Analysis Areas

- Feature depth (invoicing, expenses, payroll, taxes)
- Pricing models & packaging
- User experience & learning curve
- Bank integration capabilities
- Tax compliance automation
- Mobile experience
