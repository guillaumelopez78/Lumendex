# Strategy Hub

Strategic planning and go-to-market for **Accounting** automation initiative.

---

## 🎯 Strategic Vision

**Make accounting invisible** — Automate what can be automated, so SMB owners never have to think about bookkeeping again.

---

## 📊 Key Strategic Questions

- How do we integrate with existing accountant workflows?
- What's the minimum viable automation to save 80% of time?
- Pricing: Per transaction? Monthly subscription? Transaction + subscription?
- How do we handle multi-country tax differences?

---

## 🛣️ Roadmap Phases

### Phase 1: Smart Bank Reconciliation
- Auto-match transactions to invoices
- Smart categorization
- Reconciliation dashboard

### Phase 2: Invoice Automation  
- Email/upload capture
- Data extraction via OCR
- Auto-match to payments

### Phase 3: Compliance & Reporting
- Tax obligation calculation
- P&L generation
- Tax prep documents

### Phase 4: AI Insights
- Anomaly detection
- Tax optimization
- Spending patterns
