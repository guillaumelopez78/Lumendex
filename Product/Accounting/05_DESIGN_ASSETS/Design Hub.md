### User Flows

- Invoice capture flow
- Tax compliance checklist
- Report generation

### Wireframes & Prototypes
- Reconciliation dashboard
- Invoice upload interface
- Tax preparation wizard
- Report templates

- Component library alignment
- Accessibility (WCAG AA)
- Mobile-responsive design

## 📊 Design Principles

- **Simple:** One-click reconciliation where possible
- **Transparent:** Clear explanation of categorization logic
- **Integrated:** Native Shine banking integration
- **Compliance-friendly:** Accountant-ready exports
