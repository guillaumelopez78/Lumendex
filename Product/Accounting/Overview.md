## 🎯 One-Line Strategy

**Eliminate manual accounting** through deep Shine banking integration, automated invoice capture, and intelligent categorization—letting SMB owners focus on their business.

---

## 💡 The Problem

**Current State for SMB Owners:**
- Manual data entry from bank statements
- Invoice receipt & categorization is time-consuming
- Tax compliance is complex (VAT, income tax, deductions)
- Accounting firms charge €100–500/month
- No real-time visibility into P&L

**Market Opportunity:**
- ~2M SMBs in target markets
- 70%+ use spreadsheets or paper
- High pain → High willingness to pay for automation

---

## 🚀 Accounting Solution (Roadmap)

### Phase 1: Smart Bank Reconciliation
- Auto-match transactions to invoices
- One-click reconciliation
- Transaction categorization
- Multi-currency support

### Phase 2: Invoice Automation
- Capture invoices via email/upload
- Extract key data (amount, date, vendor)
- Auto-match to payments
- Vendor management

### Phase 3: Compliance & Reporting
- Auto-calculate tax obligations
- Generate P&L, balance sheet
- Tax preparation documents
- Export to accountant formats

### Phase 4: AI Insights
- Spending patterns & anomalies
- Tax optimization suggestions
- Cash flow forecasting integration
- Smart deduction recommendations
