## Do / Don't

| ✅ Do | ❌ Don't |
|---|---|
| Instant-effect settings | Form-based (use Checkbox) |
| Descriptive label: "Notifications" | Generic: "Enable" |
| Compact in dense lists | Large toggle in dense list |
| For permissions and access | Inside form with Save button |
