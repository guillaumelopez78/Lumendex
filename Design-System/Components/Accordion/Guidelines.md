## Do / Don't

| ✅ Do | ❌ Don't |
|---|---|
| Progressive disclosure | Hide essential info |
| Max 1 level deep | Nested accordions |
| FAQ, settings, details | Single expandable (use Disclosure) |
| Multiple open at once | Exclusive one-open-only |
